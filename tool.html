Age at Time of Invitation
Age is assessed at the date of invitation, not EOI submission. If a birthday falls between EOI and invitation, the new age applies.
English Language Proficiency
IELTS scores required (all 4 bands):
Superior English = 8.0+ in every band → 20 pts
Proficient English = 7.0+ in every band → 10 pts
Competent English = 6.0+ in every band → 0 pts (minimum eligible, no points awarded)
PTE Academic: Superior = 79+, Proficient = 65+, Competent = 50+
Note: As of Aug 7 2025, remote-proctored tests are not accepted.

💡 IELTS 7→8 adds 10 points — highest single boost available to most applicants

Educational Qualifications
Official DHA point allocations:
PhD / Doctorate = 20 pts
Masters degree = 15 pts (same as Bachelor under DHA rules — many calculators get this wrong)
Bachelor degree = 15 pts
Diploma or trade qualification = 10 pts
Points are only awarded for qualifications assessed as relevant to your nominated occupation.
Overseas Work Experience
Important rules:
• Must be in your nominated skilled occupation (or closely related)
• Must be at least 20 hours per week, paid employment
• Only experience in the last 10 years counts
• Combined cap: Overseas + Australian work experience cannot exceed 20 points total
Australian Work Experience
Australian experience scores more per year than overseas. Combined overseas + Australian total is capped at 20 pts. Same rules apply: nominated occupation, 20+ hrs/week paid, last 10 years.
⚠️ Your overseas + Australian work experience exceeds the 20-point cap. Your work experience total has been capped at 20 pts as per DHA rules.
Partner / Spouse
Skilled partner (10 pts) — Partner must be: included in your application + hold a positive skills assessment in an eligible occupation + have competent English + meet age requirements.
Single / partner is Aus citizen or PR (10 pts) — Also 10 pts.
Partner without skills assessment (0 pts) — No points if partner doesn't meet the criteria.
Bonus Points — Claim All That Apply
Nomination Points
You can only hold one nomination at a time. 190 state nomination and 491 regional nomination are mutually exclusive — claim only one.
