What counts as "Regional Australia"?

For the 491 visa, regional Australia means anywhere in Australia EXCEPT Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Gold Coast, Newcastle and Lake Macquarie, Illawarra (Wollongong), Sunshine Coast, Perth and Geelong — and their surrounding major urban areas.

Important: Most of Australia is "regional" under the 491 definition. All capital cities except Sydney, Melbourne and Brisbane are included in many regional zone definitions. Adelaide, Hobart, Darwin and Canberra are all either fully or mostly regional eligible.

491 → 191 Permanent Residency — What You Need
The 491 is a 5-year provisional visa, not permanent residency. After 3 years of living and working in regional Australia, you can apply for the Subclass 191 Permanent Residence visa — which is permanent with no conditions.
191 RequirementDetail
3 years regional residenceMust have lived in a designated regional area for at least 3 years while holding the 491 visa. This is verified — DHA checks location records.
Income thresholdMust have earned at least the minimum income threshold (approximately $53,900/year) during the 3-year period. This is to ensure genuine regional workforce contribution.
Competent EnglishIELTS 6.0 all bands minimum at time of 191 application.
Health and characterFull health examination and police clearances again — same as initial 491 application.
Complied with 491 visa conditionsMust have genuinely complied with regional living and working conditions throughout the 3-year period. Breaches can affect 191 eligibility.
After 191 PR: You receive permanent residency with no location conditions. You can move to any city in Australia, change employers freely, and live exactly as any other permanent resident. After 4 years of permanent residency (1 year as citizen) you can apply for Australian citizenship.
Why 491 is the fastest PR pathway for most profiles scoring 60–80 points
Accountant at 70 base points — 491 is the answer
189 cutoff for accountants: 95 pts — essentially impossible. 190 cutoff for accountants (SA): 65 pts, + 5 pts = 70 pts base needed. But 491 SA: 65 pts base + 15 = 80 pts effective. SA accepts accountants for 491 with only 65 base points. This is why most Pakistani and Indian accountants in Australia pursue SA 491 as their primary pathway.
Software engineer at 75 base points
189 cutoff: 90+ pts. 190 VIC cutoff: ~85 pts effective. 491: 75 base + 15 = 90 effective. In regional areas (Canberra ACT, Adelaide SA, Ballarat VIC), software engineers have strong employment markets comparable to capital cities — with the 491 making the visa significantly easier to achieve.
Student with 60 base points at graduation
Regional study during your degree adds +5 points. If you also get regional nomination (491), that's +15 more = 60 + 5 + 15 = 80 effective points. For many healthcare and education graduates, 491 combined with regional study makes PR achievable within 3–4 years of starting university.
