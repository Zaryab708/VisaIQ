The NAATI Credentialled Community Language (CCL) test adds 5 points to your DHA score — the same as a full year of Australian work experience. If your language is on the NAATI list, this is the highest-value single action you can take before graduation. $800 and 3 months of preparation.

Section 5
Regional study advantage calculator
Two separate DHA bonuses — both may apply to you:
Australian study bonus (+5 pts) — requires 2 academic years in Australia in a CRICOS-registered course for your occupation. Available to metro and regional students.
Regional study (+5 pts) — requires 2 academic years at a regional campus. Cannot be claimed alongside the Australian study bonus for the same degree — claim whichever applies (usually regional gives the same 5 pts but unlocks the 491 pathway).
491 regional nomination (+15 pts) — separate and stackable. Requires living and working in regional Australia for 3 years after nomination. Does not require regional study — but regional study significantly improves 491 nomination access.

Section 8
Work rights and 485 intelligence
Work rights — 500 Student Visa (DHA Condition 8105)
While your course is IN SESSION
Maximum 48 hours per fortnight. Applies during all teaching periods, exam periods, and any period you are enrolled in subjects counting toward your degree. Applies across all employers combined — including unpaid work that would normally attract payment.
During official course breaks and holidays
Unlimited work hours. No cap applies when your course is not in session — semester breaks, summer holidays, mid-semester breaks. The 48-hour limit resumes the moment your next teaching period begins.
Masters by Research and PhD students
No work hour restriction at any time once your course has commenced. Applies to research-stream degrees only — not coursework Masters. Confirm your stream via VEVO or your visa grant notice.
The July 2023 change increased the cap from 40 to 48 hours per fortnight during study periods. It did not remove the course break exemption — unlimited hours during official breaks has always applied and continues to apply in 2025-26.

One fortnight = 14 consecutive days starting on a Monday. Calculate rolling 14-day periods — not just week by week. You can breach the cap across a fortnight even if each individual week looks fine.

Mandatory CRICOS-registered work placements are exempt from the 48-hour cap. Check if your course has a work component listed on CRICOS.

Source: DHA Condition 8105 — immi.homeaffairs.gov.au · Verify your specific conditions via VEVO.
