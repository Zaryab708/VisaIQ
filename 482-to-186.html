186 TRT Processing Time Currently 6–18 months from application lodgement. Lodge your 186 as soon as your employer nominates you — there is no benefit to waiting. You can continue working on your 482 while the 186 is processing.
186 TRT Eligibility Requirements
✓ 2 years sponsored work in nominated occupation Across one or more approved sponsors. Employment must be continuous and full-time (or part-time equivalent).
✓ Under 45 years at time of nomination Age is checked when your employer lodges the 186 nomination — not when you started on 482. Exceptions exist for medical practitioners, academics and some scientists earning $141K+.
✓ Competent English — IELTS 6.0 all bands Higher than the 482 requirement of 5.0. If you are a native speaker (UK, US, NZ, Canada, Ireland) you are exempt.
✓ Positive skills assessment Required for most occupations. If you already held one for your 482 application, check whether it is still current (usually valid 3 years).
✓ Employer must nominate you Your employer lodges the 186 nomination — you cannot self-nominate. The employer pays the $540 nomination fee and must still meet Labour Market Testing requirements.
⚠️ Work must be with approved sponsors only From 29 November 2025, only employment with businesses that hold approved Standard Business Sponsorship counts toward your 2-year requirement. Employment with non-approved entities does not count.
Don't want to wait for your employer? Alternative PR pathways
Points-tested PR (189 / 190 / 491) — independent of employer
While on your 482, you can simultaneously build your points score and apply for a points-tested skilled visa. If your occupation is on the MLTSSL, you do not need your employer's involvement. Check your PR points score using the VisaIQ PR Tool.

494 Regional Employer Sponsored — if your employer is regional
If your employer operates in a regional area of Australia, you may be eligible for the 494 visa instead, which leads to 191 PR after 3 years in regional Australia.

How the 482 → 186 PR pathway works
1
Work 2 years on 482 with approved sponsor(s)
Reduced from 3 years in December 2024. Can be across multiple employers — all must be approved Standard Business Sponsors.
2
Employer lodges 186 nomination
Your employer nominates you for permanent residency. $540 employer fee. Must still conduct Labour Market Testing.
3
You lodge 186 visa application
$4,770 fee. Health checks, police clearances, updated skills assessment if required. You can lodge as soon as employer nomination is lodged.
✓
Permanent Residency granted — 6 to 18 months later
You receive your 186 PR visa. You can then live and work anywhere in Australia, sponsor family members, and apply for citizenship after 4 years of permanent residency (1 year as citizen).
