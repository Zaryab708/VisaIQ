2026 Policy update
Onshore vs offshore.
Your report knows the difference.

The 2026 policy changes significantly affect which pathway is fastest depending on whether you are currently in Australia or overseas. Your report accounts for this.

🇦🇺
Onshore — priority processing
If you are in Australia on a 485 graduate visa, bridging visa, or student visa — you now receive priority consideration in state nomination rounds. SA and TAS explicitly favour onshore applicants in 2026. Your report reflects this advantage in state recommendation logic.
✈️
Offshore — fewer options, still viable
Offshore applicants are no longer prioritised in most state nomination rounds. However, QLD 190 still accepts offshore applicants for certain occupations. The 189 federal pathway remains available regardless of location. Your report maps exactly which states and pathways are open to you.
⚠️
What most people miss
Many offshore applicants lodge 190 ROIs for states that have quietly stopped accepting offshore applications. This wastes months. Your report shows the current status for each state for your specific occupation — saving you from applying where you cannot be considered.
